Description             : Four classic style vanilla Doom episodes!
                          36 Action packed maps!
                          Challenging trap filled gameplay
                          Operation Obsolete - Doomguy finds himself in an
                          abandoned run down UAC base over-run with
                          hellspawn!
                          Crude Reserve - This terrestrial wasteland is now a
                          sea of Oil! Escape the toxic polluted grounds of
                          Petroleum fueled terror!
                          Lethal Sea - The name says it all! Lost at sea and
                          marooned in shores of hellish terror!
                          The Darkest Void - Your Mind Frayed! Doomguys
                          toughest challenge in this adventure! Escape the
                          encroaching black hole that beckons...
                          Can you find my secret maps..?
