Game                    : Ultimate Doom
Single Player           : Designed for

Difficulty Settings     : Yes


* Construction *

Base                    : New from scratch
Build Time              : Originally 2 years on and off. About 8 months on
                          and off for the 10th anniversary edition.
Editor(s) used          : Various
May Not Run With...     : Vanilla doom.exe because the music in the WAD is
